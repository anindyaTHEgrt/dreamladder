ABOUT US

Dreamladder Investment Advisors Pvt Ltd was founded by Sanjeev B Zarbade in 2021 after his 16 year stint with Kotak Securities to offer investment management services. We obtained a Registered Investment Advisory (RIA) license from SEBI in 2021. Our core offering is non discretionary PMS services and advisory services.

We are a research-focused firm that endeavors to provide long term capital appreciation on a risk-adjusted basis by identifying mispriced companies. We prefer to invest in asset-light companies with a good management track record of efficient capital allocation and those that deliver on their execution plans.
